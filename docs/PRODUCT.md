# FoodCal — Product Overview

**FoodCal** is a simple, friendly web app that helps you understand and manage what
you eat. Snap a photo of your meal or just type what you had, and FoodCal estimates
the calories and nutrition for you. Set a goal, track your weight, and get gentle,
personalized coaching to stay on track.

It's built for Indian and South Indian food in particular, but works for everyday
meals too.

🔗 **Live app:** https://foodcal.vercel.app

---

## Who it's for

Anyone who wants an easy way to keep an eye on their eating — whether your goal is
to lose weight, gain weight, or simply eat more mindfully — without the hassle of
searching long food databases or weighing everything.

---

## How it works (in a nutshell)

1. **Sign in** with your email and password.
2. **Set up your plan** — tell FoodCal a few details and your goal, and it works out a
   safe daily calorie target for you.
3. **Log your food** throughout the day by typing it in or taking a photo.
4. **Check in on your weight** and watch your progress on a simple chart.
5. **End your day** to get a quick, encouraging summary of how you're doing.

## Features

### 📸 Log food by photo or text
- **Take a photo** of your plate with your phone's camera, right inside the app, or
  **upload an existing picture**. FoodCal looks at the image and estimates what's on
  the plate and how many calories it has.
- Prefer typing? Just write something like **"2 idlis"** or **"1 masala dosa"** and get
  an instant estimate.
- Already know the numbers? **Add an item manually** — just type the food name and its
  calories and it goes straight into your log, no estimate needed.

### ⚖️ Adjust the portion
Ate more or less than the standard serving? Before saving a food, nudge the
**servings** up or down (e.g. ×2) and the calories and nutrition update instantly.

### 🥗 Calorie & nutrition breakdown
Every estimate shows the **calories** plus a **macro breakdown** — Protein, Carbs,
Fat, Fiber, and Sugar — along with how confident the app is in the estimate.

### 🎯 Your personalized plan
Tell FoodCal your **age, sex, height, weight, activity level, goal weight, and target
date** (and optionally your body-fat %). It then gives you:
- A **recommended daily calorie target** to reach your goal by your chosen date.
- Your **maintenance calories** — roughly what you'd eat to stay the same weight — so
  you can see the daily deficit (or surplus) the plan is using.
- **Daily macro targets** (protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sugar).
- Your **BMI now and at your goal weight**, with an easy-to-read category.
- A **safety check** — if your timeline is too aggressive, it suggests a healthier,
  more realistic target date instead of recommending an unsafe crash diet.

### 📈 Progress chart & weight check-ins
- Log your **weight each day** with a quick check-in.
- A clean, simple **chart** shows your **projected path** to your goal alongside your
  **actual weigh-ins**, with a "you are here" marker — so you can see at a glance
  whether you're ahead, behind, or right on track.

### 🔥 Daily goal tracker
Your daily calorie target shows up as a **countdown** — see how many calories you
have left for the day (or how far over you've gone) with a progress bar.

### 📅 Daily log & history
- Add foods to your **daily log** and remove anything you logged by mistake.
- **Browse previous days** to see what you ate and your totals for each day.

### ⏱️ Fasting window & timer
Doing intermittent fasting? Pick a window (12:12, 16:8, 18:6, 20:4, OMAD, or a custom
number of hours), tap **Start fast**, and a **live timer** counts your fasting time
with a progress bar toward your target. Tap **End fast** when you break it. Forgot to
hit start (or stop) on time? You can **enter the exact start and end times manually**.
Your fast keeps running even if you close and reopen the app, and today's fast is
included in your **End the day** review.

### 🍛 "What should I eat?" suggestions
Ask any time of day and FoodCal suggests specific **Tamil / South Indian** dishes,
snacks, or drinks to help you hit your remaining calories and macros — prioritizing
what you're short on (like protein or fiber). If you're near or over your limit, it
suggests lighter, filling options (rasam, buttermilk, sundal, and the like). On a
fresh day it offers a balanced spread for the whole day.

### 🤖 "End the day" insights
Tap **End my day** and FoodCal gives you a short, encouraging coach's note covering:
- How **today** went versus your target.
- Your **week** and **month** trends.
- Your progress toward your **goal**.
- If you ate over your maintenance level, it gently tells you roughly **how much weight
  that could add** if repeated — so there are no surprises.

### 📤 Export your data
Download your full food log any time as a **CSV** (for spreadsheets) or **JSON** file —
your data is yours to keep.

### 📱 Install it like an app
Add FoodCal to your phone's **home screen** and it opens full-screen with its own
icon, just like a native app — no app store needed.

### 🌗 Light & dark mode
Switch between light and dark themes with one tap. The app remembers your choice.

### ⚡ Fast & lightweight
FoodCal is designed to feel quick and smooth on any phone or laptop, with a clean,
uncluttered look.

## Your privacy & security

- **Your data is private.** Your food log, weight history, and plan are tied to your
  account and only visible to you.
- **Bring your own AI key.** The smart estimates are powered by OpenAI. You add your
  own OpenAI key once in **Settings**; it's **stored securely (encrypted)** and is
  **never shown back to you or sent to your browser** — it's only used behind the
  scenes to generate your estimates.
- **You're in control.** You can update your plan, clear a day, remove check-ins, or
  export your data whenever you like.

---

## Good to know

- **Estimates, not exact figures.** Calorie and nutrition numbers are AI estimates and
  can vary. They're great for guidance and trends, but they won't be lab-accurate.
- **Not medical advice.** FoodCal is a helpful tool, not a substitute for a doctor or
  dietitian. Please consult a professional before making big changes to your diet —
  especially if you have any health conditions.
- **Safe by design.** The plan never recommends an unsafe calorie level or an
  unrealistically fast pace of weight change.
